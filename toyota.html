TOYOTA
 
  • Overview

Toyota is probably the most popular and dominant brand in Zimbabwe. The make needs not much in terms of introduction. Toyotas are reputed to be strong, sturdy, and, regardless of mileage, generally score very high in terms of resale value. Toyota is one of the world's largest automakers, and the market leader in Africa. Ever since its creation in 1937, the Japanese firm has been renowned for the quality and exceptional reliability of its high-tech products. In 2011, it was ranked 8th in Fortune Magazine's list of largest corporations.Trusted network of choice for leading global brands Toyota Zimbabwe is the exclusive distributor of the prestigious global brand Toyota and Hino Trucks. It sells a wide range of new passenger vehicles and light commercial vehicles. Efficiency boosted by a strong local presence Toyota Zimbabwe offers nationwide coverage, providing customers with a responsible and reliable service. A dynamic, customer-centric organization Toyota Zimbabwe can tap into the entire logistical organization of the CFAO Group to ensure that vehicles and spare parts are readily available. It comprises a team of skilled professionals striving for customer satisfaction through a relentless focus on quality and compliance with Toyota international standards of excellence. Unstinting commitment to customer service It uses modern and spacious facilities and leading-edge technology to provide a complete range of before- and after-sales services to meet all customer needs: Manufacturers' warranty All vehicles and spare parts sold through our network are fully covered by Toyota warranty. Original spare parts Toyota Zimbabwe uses only original Toyota spare parts in order to provide the highest standards of service and to guarantee product performance over the long term. After-sales service Toyota Zimbabwe offers a comprehensive range of after-sales services thanks to leading-edge facilities and skilled personnel who receive training from Toyota on a regular basis. Autoheads advises owners to take care of their cars by getting the correct spares from reputable traders, as well as enlisting the services of qualified mechanics. Autoheads helps in ensuring that your car stays on the road.
